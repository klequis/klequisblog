Who is the blog meant for?

The target audience of this blog is people with basic to advance computer skills who want to learn to be software developers, or experienced developers exploring new technologies.

Who is Karl?

I'm an aging software developer who doen't have to work 10+ hours a day anymore and so I have time to go back to were my career with software began - exploring technology that interests and facinates me.

I have played every major role in the software process from idea generation and requirements gathering to deployment. I have been an Excel guru, software developer, software develoment manager, project manager and product manager.   • SharePoint

Applications I have build have been in the domains of user productivity, document authoring, document management, knowledge management, information overload, Investment Banking, market data and CRM.

My favorite part of the software development process is watching users make use of what I have been part of creating.

I am currently an organizer of a Bay Area Free Code Camp and a drone flyer.

Why use Ubuntu?

Why use GNOME?

Largely because the UI appeals to me more than the default Ubuntu Unity UI. I like the simplicity and the ability to customize it via shell extensions.
