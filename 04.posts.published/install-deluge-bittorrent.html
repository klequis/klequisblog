Download the Ubuntu GNOME .iso Using Deluge

Go to the download page for Ubuntu GNOME 16.04 LTS.

Under the section for Standard PC > PC 64 bit, click on the blue button that says "64 bit TORRENT FILE".

Go to the location you downloaded the file to, right-click 'ubuntu-gnome-16.04-desktop-amd64.iso.torrent' and click .

When the Windows Firewall windows appears, click .

Make sure the correct torrent file is selected and then click .

It may take a minute or two but the download will start without further action on your part. The entire download with an average cable connection takes less than 10 minutes.

If you leave Deluge open it will immediately start pulling the ISO file from your machine to other BitTorrent users. While this seems fair, when I did this a few years ago my internet provider throttled my upload connection and I had to call them to clear it up. As a result, I keep the BitTorrent client closed.

All done! You can not use the .iso to install Ubuntu GNOME.
