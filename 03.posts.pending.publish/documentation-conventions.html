This website uses the following documentation conventions

A blue callout contains information of interest.

Information in a yellow callout is critical to the completion of the tutorial.

A red callout will warn you of common mistakes or tell you what not to do.

A green callout signifies something significant has been completed.

Text in a represents a button or menu you should click on.

References: The References section contains links to resources I relied on to write the tutorial.

See Also: The See Also section contains links to related information you can use to further your understanding of the tutorial's topic.
