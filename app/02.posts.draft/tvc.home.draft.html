The Tri-Valley Coders is just getting started but the group is well established and has great momentum (or: has been around for over a year). We are 180 members strong and have had over 75 in-person meetings in our first year.

Tri-Valley Coders is a comfortable non-competitive place to learn about making software, both in person and online, for beginners and vetrans alike. Our members span the gammute in terms of age and experience, but they all have one thing in common, they are learning something new and looking to take the next step in their lives.

The groups works with the latest technolgoies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, Redux, Express and MongoDB.

At Tri-Valley Coders we are not just focused on writing code, we are making software and call ourselves 'software developers' because, while writing code is fun, we all want to make an app or service and deploy it to users.

Tri-Valle Coders is not 180 members strong. In the past 12 months we have had more than 75 in-person meetings. The normal meeting size is 5 to 10 people (which isn't a lot but then try to get individual attention in a group of 50).

You can come to one our in-person sessions for any of the following reasons

  • Get help with a secific problem
  • Get advice on what to learn, how to approach learning, best learning resources and even a bit of career advice.
  • Make connections
  • Or just be in the company of others with a common interest
