Coding is a big part of making software and there are many types of code. There is what most people mean, the language, such as C, JavaScript, Phthon ... the list is long. But there is also HTML, CSS and things such as templating languages. These are all forms of coding. And let's not forget teamwork which I'll get to in a little bit.

The Myth of the Full-Stack Developer

The term full-stack means developers who are comfortable working with both back-end and front-end technologies. To be more specific, it means that the developer can work with databases, PHP, HTML, CSS, JavaScript and everything in between, also, venturing as far as converting Photoshop designs to front-end code.

'Full-stack developer' is not completely a myth. It depends on what you are building and what technologies you are using.
